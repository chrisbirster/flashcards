Privacy Policy

Privacy for the Vutadex browser-based flashcard and study workspace

Vutadex provides a browser-first study workspace at app.vutadex.com where users create notes, generate flashcards, manage decks, and review with spaced repetition. This page explains the information Vutadex handles in order to provide the service.

Last updated

April 2, 2026

Support

sales@vutadex.com

Service

Browser-based flashcard and study workspace subscriptions.

Information Vutadex collects

Account and sign-in information

Vutadex collects the email address you use to request one-time login codes, along with basic account and workspace profile information needed to keep your session active and secure.

Study content you create

Vutadex stores the notes, cards, decks, templates, tags, settings, and study-session data you create while using the app.

Billing and plan information

If you use a paid plan, Vutadex stores subscription and billing metadata needed to manage the current workspace plan, customer status, and plan changes.

Support and operational data

Vutadex may store support emails, security logs, request logs, and operational metrics needed to troubleshoot issues, protect the service, and understand reliability.

How Vutadex uses information

  • To authenticate users and deliver one-time sign-in codes.
  • To host, sync, and present notes, cards, decks, templates, and study history.
  • To process subscriptions, plan changes, and billing events.
  • To provide support and respond to account or payment questions.
  • To monitor service health, investigate abuse, and keep the platform secure.

Service providers

Vutadex relies on third-party providers for hosting, database infrastructure, email delivery, and payment processing. Those providers only receive the information needed to perform those services.

Retention and security

Vutadex keeps account, content, and billing records for as long as they are needed to operate the service, support customers, protect the platform, and comply with legal obligations.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact sales@vutadex.com.
