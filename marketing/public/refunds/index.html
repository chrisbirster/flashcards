Cancellation / Refund Policy

Cancellation timing is defined. Final refund language still needs owner confirmation.

This page explains what the current product and billing code already support, and it calls out the few remaining policy details that still need explicit owner-approved copy before a broad public billing launch.

Cancellation policy

Paid Vutadex plans are subscription plans for the current workspace. If a cancellation or downgrade is scheduled, paid access remains active through the end of the current billing period.

Users should manage plan changes through the app billing settings or by contacting sales@vutadex.com.

What happens after cancellation

  • No immediate cutoff is promised when a paid plan is canceled.
  • Access remains through the paid term that has already been billed.
  • Future renewals stop after the current billing period ends.

Refund policy status

Owner input still needed

Confirm whether Vutadex offers refunds only for billing errors, offers discretionary refunds, or treats paid plans as non-refundable except where required by law.

Until that final policy language is confirmed, billing and charge questions should be sent to sales@vutadex.com.

If you believe a charge was made in error, include the workspace name, plan, date of charge, and the email address tied to the account.
